# What is successful

- Clear hierarchy, cohesive palette, responsive behavior, semantic structure, and a working mobile navigation.
- The illustration is original and the composition is not a generic three-card template.

# Why it does not reach the reference ceiling

- The CSS cup communicates category but not place, aroma, light, people, material craft, or neighborhood atmosphere.
- The hero art is a contained decoration rather than an immersive narrative surface.
- The composition is balanced and safe; it lacks the dramatic crop and visual tension of the stronger references.
- The supporting product imagery remains synthetic, so every section has similar visual weight.

# Corrective lesson

When the emotional promise depends on atmosphere or physical craft, escalate to supplied or generated raster imagery before implementation. Code-native illustration is appropriate only when illustration is the chosen brand medium, not merely the cheapest available substitute.
