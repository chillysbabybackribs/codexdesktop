# Why this works

- The cafe is communicated through a real place and a real material atmosphere, not decorative coffee symbols.
- The headline is the dominant visual mass and has purposeful line breaks.
- Photography, typography, rules, mask geometry, and product rail all share one editorial system.
- The first viewport contains enough product evidence to feel complete without becoming crowded.

# Risks to inspect in implementation

- The complex mask must collapse gracefully on mobile.
- Text must remain live HTML rather than being baked into the image.
- Real photography needs provenance, optimized responsive sources, fixed dimensions, and meaningful alternatives.
- The small product rail must preserve readable labels and 44px interaction targets.
