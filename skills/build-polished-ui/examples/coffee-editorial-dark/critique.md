# Why this works

- The dominant photograph communicates craft, temperature, material quality, and mood instantly.
- Copper is simultaneously a photographed material, an accent color, a border color, and an icon color.
- The display type is large enough to behave as composition rather than ordinary page copy.
- Small utilities use a distinct typographic voice without becoming generic interface chrome.

# Risks to inspect in implementation

- Cream-on-black contrast must remain readable over every responsive crop.
- Smoke and low-key photography can become muddy after compression.
- Condensed uppercase navigation requires sufficient size, spacing, and focus treatment.
- The visual concept should not be mistaken for proof of accessibility, responsive behavior, or working navigation.
