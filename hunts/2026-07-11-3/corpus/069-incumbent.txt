IMPORTANT :: What is the SuiteDash WordPress Plugin? This plugin allows you to add a customized login functionality directly on your WordPress site, and have that login tied-in to your SuiteDash account. This allows you, your staff, and your clients to login to your SuiteDash environment directly from your site, without ever needing to visit the SuiteDash login page. SuiteDash customers with a Custom URL set up are able to integrate their custom URLs with this plugin! SuiteDash is in constant development, and is updated daily with new features and streamlining.
