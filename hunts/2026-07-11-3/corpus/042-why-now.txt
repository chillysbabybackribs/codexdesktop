The new structure introduces annual and usage-based fees for third-party developers who build tools for Amazon sellers - but keeps SP-API free for sellers using it directly for their own businesses . At Tirnav Solutions , we’ve worked extensively with SP-API for high-volume sellers, analytics SaaS platforms, and automation tools . This shift impacts every third-party developer building applications for Amazon’s ecosystem. Quick Summary No change for sellers using SP-API for their own Amazon business. They can continue using SP-API free of charge. Third-party developers (offering tools to other sellers) will move to a subscription and usage-based pricing model . Core developer resources - documentation, SDKs, and sandbox testing - remain free for all. What’s Changing in 2026 1. Annual Subscription Fee - $1,400 USD Starting January 31, 2026 , all third-party developers integrating with SP-API will be required to pay an annual subscription fee . This fee covers: Access to the Solution Provider Portal Support services and developer case management Production SP-API usage New developers will be charged after their first production API call , while existing developers will begin billing automatically on this date. 2. Monthly API Usage Tiers - Effective April 30, 2026 Amazon will introduce a tiered usage model based on monthly GET API call volume . All developers start in the Basic Tier with no additional monthly charge, and can upgrade as needed. Your access remains free.
