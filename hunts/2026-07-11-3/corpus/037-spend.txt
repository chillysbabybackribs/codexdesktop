TYPE OF WORK Part Time WAGE / SALARY 5 per hour HOURS PER WEEK 30 DATE UPDATED Dec 16, 2025 JOB OVERVIEW Job Overview We are a U.S.-based construction company seeking a highly detail-oriented virtual assistant to support invoicing, payment tracking, data transcription, and basic bank reconciliation. This role is numbers-focused and spreadsheet-driven. Accuracy, consistency, and the ability to reconcile data correctly are more important than speed. Completed college degree is REQUIRED. Degree must be in accounting, bookkeeping, finance, business, or a related field. Compensation is negotiable and will be determined based on demonstrated skill level, accuracy, and test performance. This is a high-skill role. Responsibilities Prepare and track invoices Record and apply payments (including partial payments and credits) Maintain accounts receivable records Track outstanding balances and due dates Transcribe financial data from written documents or statements into spreadsheets Reconcile spreadsheet records against bank statements Identify missing, duplicate, or unmatched transactions Flag discrepancies with clear notes Provide simple weekly summaries (unpaid, overdue, issues) Required Skills Strong proficiency in spreadsheets (Excel or Google Sheets) Accurate transcription of financial data from written or statement-based sources Ability to reconcile spreadsheet totals to bank statements Solid understanding of invoicing and payment tracking High attention to detail Ability to follow written instructions exactly Reliable internet and quiet workspace Clear written English IMPORTANT – READ CAREFULLY Applications that do not follow instructions will not be reviewed. To apply: Subject line must be: INVOICE ACCURACY First line of your message must say: “I read the full post.” Answer all questions below. Application Questions Describe your experience with invoicing and payment tracking. How do you prevent duplicate invoices or duplicate payments? Explain step-by-step how you handle a partial payment on an invoice. How do you transcribe data accurately from bank statements into spreadsheets? Explain how you reconcile a spreadsheet to a bank statement. What spreadsheet functions are you comfortable using? What is your internet speed (upload/download)? Hiring Process Qualified applicants will be asked to complete a short skills test before any interviews are scheduled. Only candidates who pass testing will be invited to a brief interview. After testing and interviews, finalists will complete a standard verification step (government-issued ID and education confirmation, if applicable) before onboarding.
