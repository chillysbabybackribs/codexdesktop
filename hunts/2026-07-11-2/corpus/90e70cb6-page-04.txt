# bexio + Revolut Business

bexio + Revolut Business integrations

## Manage new transactions in Revolut Business by creating manual entries in bexio

Streamline your financial process with this automation that activates when a new transaction is posted in your Revolut Business, and in response, creates a manual entry in bexio. This workflow assists in keeping your business accounts updated, eliminating the need for manual data entry and making your business financial management more efficient. Enhance your bookkeeping process and save valuable time for your business.
