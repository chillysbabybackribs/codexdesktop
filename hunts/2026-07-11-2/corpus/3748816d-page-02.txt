## Best Unauthorized Seller Detection for E-commerce Brands

### How Unauthorized Sellers Enter the Market

#### Many brands are surprised to discover how products reach unauthorized sellers.

Common sources include:

Distribution Leakage

Products are diverted from authorized distributors and resold through unauthorized channels.

Gray Market Activity

Products intended for one region are sold in another market without authorization.

Marketplace Arbitrage

Sellers purchase products from retail channels and resell them online.

Liquidation Inventory

Excess inventory is acquired and resold without brand approval.

Third-Party Marketplace Sellers

Online marketplaces make it easy for new sellers to list products with minimal barriers.
