## Frequently asked questions

- ##### What spreadsheets should I keep for a small business?

Small businesses vary greatly, but common spreadsheets track revenue, expenses, invoices sent, payroll, timekeeping, project status, inventory, leads, growth metrics, and marketing initiatives.

- ##### How to lock or protect an Excel spreadsheet?

To protect an online Excel spreadsheet, select the Review tab and click Manage protection. In the sidebar, protect the sheet to lock all cells. You can unlock specific cells and ranges and allow users to do specific tasks like sorting. For more security, set passwords to open the spreadsheet or edit cells.

- ##### Can AI help save time on financial management for my business?

Create your business budgets, invoices, balance sheets, among others in Excel, then utilize Copilot to automate calculations, suggest optimizations, and refine budgets, making financial management faster and more efficient.
