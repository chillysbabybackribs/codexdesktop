# Coaching software pricing, compared

the quick answer

Online coaching software starts around USD 9 to 39 per month for a few clients and scales by client count or flat tier; mid tiers for 20 to 50 clients run roughly USD 59 to 99. Below is every major platform's real, vendor-sourced pricing in one table - entry price, mid tier, free plans, and whether a branded app is included. Coachway pricing is EUR 69 per month for up to 5 clients, then EUR 9 per additional client.

Every figure comes from the vendor's own pricing page, fetched June 2026 (source linked in each row). Currencies are shown as each vendor lists them and are not converted. Where a vendor hides pricing behind a demo, that is stated, not guessed. Always confirm the current rate on the vendor page before deciding.
