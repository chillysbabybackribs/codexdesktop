## What is Employee Onboarding & Offboarding?

The Employee Onboarding & Offboarding add-in eliminates paperwork and redundancy and focuses on optimizing information as per your organizational practices. You can automate processes, assign tasks, and foster collaboration among different departments. The add-in also helps in improving overall business efficiency and profitability by eliminating the need for manual follow-ups and task assignment. All managed in a secure Microsoft environment.
