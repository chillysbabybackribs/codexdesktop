# Client Onboarding Template

This Client Onboarding Template helps you gather client goals, expectations, and preferences, setting a strong foundation for your coaching journey.

## What Is the Client Onboarding Template?

The Client Onboarding Template is a structured tool that helps coaches streamline the process of welcoming new clients. It captures key details such as client goals, challenges, session preferences, and first-session expectations. By organizing everything in one place, it ensures a smooth start, builds trust, and sets the tone for a productive coaching relationship.

## How to Use This Template

Collect client details

Record name, contact information, and background.

Define goals and expectations

Clarify what the client wants to achieve through coaching.

Schedule sessions

Note preferred days, times, and session frequency.

Prepare for the first session

Ask clients what they hope to accomplish and note key discussion points.

## Why Coaches Use This Template

Creates a professional first impression

Streamlines onboarding by keeping all client details in one place

Builds alignment between coach and client from the start
