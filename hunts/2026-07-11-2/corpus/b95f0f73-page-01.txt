## Do You Really Know Where and How Your Products Are Being Sold?

As a brand, you control your message… but not always how it’s distributed.Slashed prices, outdated visuals, or inconsistent product listings published by sellers outside your authorized distribution network can seriously damage your brand image.That’s why we built a tool that monitors the entire web—so you know exactly who is selling your products, how they’re doing it, and at what price.You may have already encountered these situations:

### Unauthorized Resellers

Unknown sellers list your products on marketplaces without your consent—often at aggressive prices, with poor customer service, and at the risk of harming your brand’s reputation.

### Copy-Paste or Inaccuracies

Some product listings reuse outdated, poorly translated, or incorrect content, distorting your brand message, creating confusion, and weakening the credibility of your offerings.

### MSRP / MAP Violations

Significant discrepancies between suggested and displayed prices undermine your pricing strategy, erode your partners’ margins, and damage trust across your distribution network.

### Internal Unfair Competition

When authorized resellers face lower prices on parallel channels, it leads to commercial tensions, loss of motivation, and potentially broken business relationships.
