## What does HR software do for small businesses?

The benefits of HR software for small businesses vary by platform, but most solutions help automate and simplify core human resources functions, such as:

- Payroll and tax compliance

- Employee onboarding and applicant tracking

- Time tracking and scheduling

- Benefits administration

- Performance and engagement

Here’s a closer look at HR tools for small businesses:

### Payroll and tax compliance

Manually processing payroll can be time-consuming and may increase the risk of errors. HR software streamlines this task by automating payroll calculations, deductions, and payment of federal, state, and local taxes.

During tax season, online HR software can also quickly produce compliant W-2 and 1099 forms. Some automatically update when tax, wage, or overtime laws change, helping your business stay compliant throughout the year.

### Employee onboarding and applicant tracking

Recruiting and hiring talented candidates is critical for keeping a small business running smoothly. HR software with applicant tracking systems (ATS) can automate job postings, collect resumes and applications, and screen candidates to compile shortlists faster.

Background check platforms, like Checkr, make it easy to run screenings for criminal history, work and education verification, and other background checks before finalizing a job offer. During onboarding, digital tools can assist with I-9 verification, document collection, and new hire checklists, helping you bring employees up to speed quickly.

### Time tracking and scheduling

Maintaining optimal staffing helps ensure efficiency and exceptional customer experience. Online HR software makes it easier to generate and share schedules and adjust them as needed.

Automated time tracking tools record clock-ins and clock-outs, manage PTO and overtime, and notify supervisors about missed punches or overtime thresholds. These features refine labor management and reduce timekeeping errors.

### Benefits administration

Offering benefits to employees can boost loyalty and retention, and the right small business HRIS software can make your program easier to manage. Benefits administration tools track eligibility, display plans, and allow employees to self-enroll or make changes during onboarding or open enrollment periods.

By connecting your benefits administration and payroll software, you can ensure the appropriate deductions are applied automatically, keeping everything in sync.

### Performance and engagement dashboards

Monitoring performance and engagement levels help small businesses retain talent and support growth. HR platforms have dashboards that track key metrics, including time-off requests and new hire retention rates, and gather feedback through surveys and reviews. Some software can even identify employees who might benefit from coaching or who are at risk of burnout, prompting managers to take proactive steps.
