## Most surprising directional finding

The largest apparent wave—AI agent tooling—was the least defensible opportunity. Formation volume was enormous, but after independence, saturation, platform-dependency, and concrete-job checks, it produced no qualifying intersection. The sharper signals were two old-fashioned approval/reconciliation workflows, yet each remained a single-source anecdote rather than a market-backed wedge.

## Fact that would change the result

A second commercial snapshot plus two independent firsthand operators paying for the same exact WATCH job—and two commercially moving products tied to it—could turn a WATCH into a GO. Until then, inventing a product would violate the evidence gate.
