# Phase 0 directions

## Accelerating commercial clusters

None defensibly established. TrustMRR supplied point-in-time 30-day growth fields, but this run created the first snapshot and captured only 100 of 8,272 rank-ordered products. Those values cannot establish 7/30/90/180-day longitudinal acceleration.

## Early formation clusters

- **AI agent/model operations:** 22 commercial-motion, 149 formation/feedback, and 39 buyer-reality lexical matches. This is launch-heavy and highly saturated; concrete paid job joins were weak.
- **Content/marketing operations:** 26 commercial-motion, 29 formation/feedback, and 34 buyer-reality lexical matches. One precise white-label asset intake/validation workflow carries a stated $1,000/year budget (`br-0147`), but independent corroboration is missing.
- **Payment/revenue operations:** 2 commercial-motion, 1 formation/feedback, and 19 buyer-reality lexical matches. One founder reports three hours monthly splitting $15K across a cofounder, contractors, affiliates, and tax (`br-0177`); this does not yet meet the two-firsthand-record requirement.

## Launch-heavy or revenue-light

- AI agent infrastructure and developer tooling: large HN formation volume, founder echo, clone density, and revocable model/platform dependencies.
- Generic content, social scheduling, and creator tools: commercial supply is broad, but exact operational jobs fragment across unrelated actors.

## Saturated or declining

- AI coding/agent wrappers: clone saturation penalty applies.
- Generic compliance automation: mature funded incumbents and no newly verified gap.
- Generic commerce/website tooling: incumbent density plus overlap with prior hunt seeds.

## Operational-residue hypotheses retained as WATCH

1. White-label SaaS teams repeatedly collect, validate, preview, approve, and publish customer branding assets. Gap fill must find two additional firsthand operators, exact budgets, and current tool failures outside HN.
2. Small software businesses repeatedly split revenue among founders, contractors, affiliates, and tax reserves. Gap fill must verify independent operators, actual paid workarounds, regulatory constraints, and at least two motion products serving this exact job.

## Evidence gaps and downstream directives

- Establish a second TrustMRR snapshot before making acceleration claims; later compare 7/30/90-day deltas.
- Resume full TrustMRR pagination after rate reset; the current 1.21% top-heavy slice is not market coverage.
- Expand buyer reality beyond HN/Stack Exchange into occupational forums, public services/jobs, paid templates, and verified Reddit threads.
- For each WATCH, require three independent domains, two actor types, two firsthand records, and exact-job money before candidate invention.
- Do not proceed to Phase 1 from this run: no direction satisfies the hard `GO` gate.
