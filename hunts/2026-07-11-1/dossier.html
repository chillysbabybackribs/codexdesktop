W-9 Chaseboard

Confidence B

“Excellent for tracking 1099's required to be issued for my business and tracks receipts of the W-9's I received!”

Demand & money

5,000+ Etsy category items; the reviewed tracker is $27.99 and adjacent sheets reach $97.

Incumbent weakness

Filing suites handle the endpoint; small firms still chase missing vendor data before filing.

V1

Import vendors, request W-9s securely, flag missing/stale records and threshold exceptions, export a preparer packet.

Why now

2026 shifts the common threshold from $600 to $2,000 while 10+ aggregate returns require e-filing.

Kill fact: 15 interviews find no remaining email/spreadsheet chase because AP/accounting tools already solve collection reliably.
