# Email Campaign Tracker Spreadsheet Template

Keeps track of email marketing campaigns, including recipients, send dates, and performance metrics.

Email Campaign Tracker

Purpose: | This spreadsheet helps track email marketing campaigns, their recipients, scheduling dates, and performance metrics like open and click rates.

Campaign Overview

Campaign Name: | {campaignName}

Campaign Owner: | {campaignOwner}

Start Date: | {startDate}

End Date: | {endDate}

Target Audience: | {targetAudience}

Campaign Details

Recipient Name | Email | Date Sent | Opened | Clicked | Open Rate | Click Rate

{#recipients.name} | {#recipients.email} | {#recipients.dateSent} | {#recipients.opened} | {#recipients.clicked} | {#recipients.openRate} | {#recipients.clickRate}

Performance Summary

Total Emails Sent | =COUNTA(recipients[Email])

Total Opens | =SUM(recipients[Opened])

Total Clicks | =SUM(recipients[Clicked])

Average Open Rate | =AVERAGE(recipients[Open Rate])

Average Click Rate | =AVERAGE(recipients[Click Rate])
