## What appointment scheduling software is

Appointment scheduling software is a tool that lets customers book appointments online through a scheduling page, booking link, or website embed. It usually connects to a calendar, displays available times, sends confirmations, and helps businesses manage upcoming appointments without manual coordination.

Instead of asking, “What time works for you?” over email or phone, the customer chooses an available time. The system confirms the appointment, updates the calendar, and can send automated reminders before the meeting.

Common features include calendar sync, Google Calendar integration, SMS reminders, automated email reminders, online payments, client management, team scheduling, recurring appointments, and custom booking pages.

Pro Tip:Your booking page should work like a landing page. The headline, service choices, form fields, confirmation message, and mobile experience all influence whether someone finishes the booking.
