No-show prevention

## Reduce no-shows with automated reminders

No-shows cost small businesses time and money. An unexpected empty slot disrupts the day you carefully planned and makes revenue unpredictable.

Acuity prevents the most common cause of no-shows: forgetting. The moment a client books, they receive a confirmation. Before their appointment, a reminder follows—by email, by text, or both. You decide the timing, write the message once, and Acuity handles the rest. For clients who have a pattern of cancelling late, requiring a deposit or a card on file at booking changes the dynamic quickly.

- Automated email confirmations sent immediately at booking

- SMS text reminders before appointments (Standard plan and above)

- Require deposits or prepayment to secure bookings

- Cancellation policies accepted by clients at the time of booking

- Clients can self-reschedule within the windows you allow

Payments & deposits

## Accept payments and deposits at booking

Chasing payment after an appointment is an awkward extra step. Acuity connects with Stripe, Square, and PayPal so you can collect payment at the point of booking, require a deposit to hold the slot, or process payment in person after the appointment. Whatever fits your business, Acuity supports it.

Sell service packages to lock in repeat business. Offer gift certificates that bring new clients through the door. Add tip prompts at checkout so gratuity is easy for clients to include. Every transaction ties to its appointment automatically, so your records stay clean without any manual work on your end.

- Accept credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash

- Require deposits or full prepayment at booking

- In-person payments via tap-to-pay and card readers

- Sell service packages, memberships, and gift certificates

- Automatic receipts sent to clients after every transaction
