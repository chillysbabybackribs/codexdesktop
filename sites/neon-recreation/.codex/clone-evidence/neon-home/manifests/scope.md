- In-scope route: public homepage only, unauthenticated, English, dark theme.
- Destination: `/tmp/codexdesktop-neon-demo`, a disposable standalone React/Vite prototype.
- Visual reference: announcement bar, sticky navigation, media-led hero, oversized low-weight type, pill actions, horizontal capability strip, alternating black/mint feature stories, grid-based product diagrams, and expanded mobile navigation.
